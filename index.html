Morning Brief

A personal daily briefing tool for Mike Cain, founder of DockeTT.

What it is

Morning Brief is a personal automation that runs on Mike Cain's Mac mini at 5:00 AM Central Time each day. It reads Mike's Gmail inboxes and Google Calendar, drafts replies to warm introductions and meeting requests, and composes a single HTML briefing email delivered to Mike.

Who it's for

This application is a single-user tool. It is not a product, service, or platform offered to others. It exists solely to automate Mike Cain's personal morning workflow.

What it does

  • Reads unread and awaiting-reply email threads from Mike's Google accounts
  • Reads Mike's Google Calendar to find today's events and open meeting slots
  • Creates draft reply emails in Mike's Gmail Drafts folder for review
  • Sends one daily HTML briefing email to Mike
